Multiple Integration with Scipy¶

  • dblquad: can be used to evaluate $\displaystyle\int_a^b\int_c^df(x,y)dxdy$
  • tplquad: can be used to evaluate $\displaystyle\int_a^b\int_c^d\int_e^ff(x,y,)dxdydz$
  • nquad: can be used to evaluate $\displaystyle\int\dots\int_Df(\mathbf{x})d\mathbf{x}$

These functions are wrappers around the single-variable quadrature function quad, which is called repeatedly along each dimension of the multiple integral

We can also compute integrals of the following form: $$ \displaystyle\int_a^b\int_{g(x)}^{h(x)}f(x,y)dxdy $$

**Example**: evaluate $$ \int_{0}^1\int_{g(x)}^{h(x)} e^{-x^2-y^2} dxdy $$

We consider two cases:

  1. $g(x)=0$ and $h(x)=1$, i.e., $\displaystyle \int_{0}^1\int_{0}^{1} e^{-x^2-y^2} dxdy $
  2. $g(x)=x-1$ and $h(x)=1-x$, i.e., $\displaystyle \int_{0}^1\int_{x-1}^{1-x} e^{-x^2-y^2} dydx $

First, let's see what we are doing here

We can also compute integrals of the following form: $$ \displaystyle\int\dots\int_Df(\mathbf{x})d\mathbf{x} $$

**Example**: evaluate $$ \int_{0}^1\dots\int_{0}^1 e^{-x_1^2-x_2^2-\dots -x_n^2} dx_1dx_2\dots dx_n $$

How to use:

integrate.nquad(f,[(a1,b1),(a_2,b2),....,(an,bn)])
